Sabre Stone Commercial

Sabre Stone Commercial, part of Sabre Stone Ltd, specialises in providing bespoke porcelain products to meet the demands of commercial clients. With a reputation for exceptional craftsmanship and innovative design, we offer tailored solutions for the hospitality industry, including high-quality tables and worktops. Our main material, sourced from Laminam, is renowned for its durability and aesthetic appeal, making it ideal for various hospitality environments, from elegant hotel dining areas to sophisticated conference spaces.

Located at Home Farm in the Baynards Park Estate, Cranleigh, Surrey, our facility is equipped to fabricate and fit a wide range of products. As we expand into the commercial sector, our experienced team continues to serve the domestic market with expertly crafted worktops. We take pride in delivering outstanding results that blend functionality and style, setting a new standard in both interior and exterior applications.

This new venture allows us to continue building on our success while diversifying our offerings to better meet the needs of our commercial clients. Sabre Stone Commercial is dedicated to providing luxurious yet practical solutions that enhance the ambiance of any space, offering unparalleled elegance and quality to the hospitality sector.
