Interior and Exterior Cladding

Porcelain cladding

Porcelain cladding offers a durable and versatile solution for interior and exterior applications. Its modern look, combined with high resistance to weather and wear, makes it a top choice for various projects. With finishes that replicate natural materials like marble, stone, and wood, porcelain cladding provides sophisticated aesthetics with the technical benefits of porcelain, ensuring longevity and minimal maintenance in both contemporary and traditional settings.

For interior spaces, porcelain cladding creates a seamless, elegant finish ideal for walls, columns, and feature areas. Its resistance to moisture and stains makes it perfect for bathrooms, kitchens, and living spaces, offering a practical yet stylish solution. Lightweight and easy to install, porcelain cladding minimizes disruption during renovations or new construction.

On exterior facades, porcelain cladding stands out for its durability and resistance to harsh weather, UV exposure, and pollutants. Acting as a protective barrier, it enhances energy efficiency by reducing thermal conductivity. With various colors and textures available, it allows for creative design while maintaining a polished, enduring appearance.

Materials

Exterior Walls

Porcelain cladding offers exceptional durability and sophisticated style, making it perfect for exterior applications. Resistant to weather and wear, it ensures long-lasting beauty while providing a sleek and modern finish. With a variety of textures and colors, our porcelain cladding solutions are designed to elevate the appearance and performance of building facades.

Interior Walls

Porcelain cladding is ideal for interior wall applications, combining elegance with practicality. Its resistance to moisture and stains makes it a popular choice for feature walls in lobbies, dining areas, and high-traffic spaces. Available in a range of finishes, it adds a touch of luxury while maintaining a clean and polished look throughout interiors.

Flooring

Porcelain flooring delivers a refined look and unmatched durability. Its non-porous and scratch-resistant surface is designed to withstand heavy foot traffic, making it a smart choice for commercial spaces and luxurious settings. With a wide selection of patterns and finishes, porcelain flooring enhances both contemporary and classic interiors.
